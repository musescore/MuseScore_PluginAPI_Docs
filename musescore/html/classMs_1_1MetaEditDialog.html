Detailed Description

Dialog for editing metatags.

NOTE: Right now, builtin metatags cannot be deleted by the user, because they are automatically created when a MasterScore is instantiated. This means that if they get deleted, they are simply readded (but empty) when the score is reopened. see also MasterScore::MasterScore()
